What is Activity Monitor?

Activitiy monitor is a webpage that allows anyone to track their personal habits.
We hope that Activity Monitor can aid in helping users remain accountable for habits
and to promote a positive lifestyle and reach their goals.

What am I able to track?

Anything! Activity Monitor is designed for users to track whatever they wish
to track, being to studying a Japanese 1 hour a day, to wanting to lose 20 pounds
over 5 months. We have predefined units and timescales so you can spend less time
planning and more time completing your goals!.

Who made Activity Monitor?

Activity Monitor was designed, created and deployed as a collaborative group project
as part of lap 2 of the training course of futureproof. The project occured over the
the span of 1 week and was created by team Ultra-Instinct.
